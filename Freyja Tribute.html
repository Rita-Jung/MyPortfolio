Freyja, Vanadis, Lady of Folkvangr

Goddess of beauty, sex, war, magic, foresight, & death

The Many Aspects of Frejya

  • The name Freyja translates to "Lady"
  • Some scholars are of the opinion that Freyja and Frigg are the same goddess, while others believe they are separate entities
  • "Vanadis" is one of Freyja's names
  • Freyja is a völva, a Norse witch
  • A völva practices an ancient Norse magical art called Seidr, which involves both the foresight of and shaping of fate.
  • Freyja presides over passion, pleasure, beauty, and fine material possessions
  • In one of her mythological stories, Loki attempts to publically humiliate Freyja by accussing her of sleeping with all the gods. Her father, Njord, jumps to her defense by stating that it is healthy for a woman to seek out pleasure.
